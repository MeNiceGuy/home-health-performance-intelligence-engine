# Home Health Performance Intelligence Engine

## 🚀 What This Is
A decision intelligence platform designed for home health agencies to identify performance gaps, improve HHVBP outcomes, and drive measurable financial impact.

This tool transforms raw operational inputs into:
- Executive-level performance reports
- Risk and efficiency diagnostics
- ROI-driven improvement strategies
- Actionable 90-day plans

---

## 💡 Who This Is For
- Home Health Agency Owners
- Administrators & Directors
- Healthcare Consultants
- Operators under HHVBP pressure

---

## 📊 Core Capabilities

### Executive Intelligence Reports
- Performance scoring (0–100)
- Risk classification (Low / Moderate / At Risk)
- Operational bottleneck detection
- Strategic recommendations

### Simulation Engine
- Scenario modeling (e.g., reduce readmissions)
- Score impact analysis
- Payment impact projections

### Operational Diagnostics
- Staffing & workload insights
- Documentation efficiency analysis
- Care coordination gaps
- Patient experience drivers

### Financial Insight
- Revenue pressure visibility
- ROI projections for improvements
- Cost vs performance alignment

---

## 🧠 Why It Matters
Most agencies operate without a clear, data-driven decision framework.

This system answers:
- Where are we losing money?
- What is hurting our HHVBP score?
- What should we fix first?
- What will actually move the needle?

The system generates a consulting-grade report including:
